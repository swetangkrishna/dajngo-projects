About Us

Acme Therapeutics (I) Pvt. Ltd was Established in 2001 as a result of the dedicated efforts put in by a team of committed professionals with strong determination to serve Humanity. We are a leading pharmaceutical marketing company including veterinary, We are involved in the marketing & export of pharmaceuticals and veterinary Medicines along with feed suppliments.

Acme Therapeutics (I) Pvt. Ltd are marketing a wide range of Pharmaceutical and veterinary products including Tablets, Injection, Capsules, Syrup, Bolus, feed supplements, I.V. FLUIDS. These products are procured from the certified and authentic manufactures of the market, who hold vast experience in this domain. Most of our manufactures are WHO-GMP certified units.

We strive to become a knowledge driven Pharmaceutical company with highest level of operational excellence in all spheres of life. Our core endeavors are to add value to customers and maximize the strength of customer relationships. Acme Therapeutics (I) Pvt. Ltd provides the effective solutions for your pharmaceutical needs. We embrace and apply the requirements of Good Manufacturing and Good Distribution Practice. Within industry, our consultants provide Quality Assurance and Regulatory Products to global and small pharmaceutical manufacturers & transportation companies.
